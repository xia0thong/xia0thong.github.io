About Justin Bieber

Quick facts. Fan-made; not affiliated.

Justin Bieber (born March 1, 1994) is a Canadian singer who broke through globally with his debut studio album My World 2.0 (2010) and its hit single “Baby.” Subsequent albums include Believe (2012), Purpose (2015), Changes (2020), and Justice (2021).

He’s known for pop and R&B across collaborations and solo hits like “Sorry,” “Love Yourself,” and “Peaches.”
